# Hair Aura Image Prompts

## Hero Image Prompts (3)

### Character Consistency Lock (Use for all hero generations)
Use your seed subject reference image: "a photo". Keep the exact same woman in all three hero images.

Identity lock rules:
- Same face structure, same skin tone, same age range, same ethnicity.
- Keep facial features consistent (eyes, nose, lips, cheekbones, jawline).
- Keep body proportions consistent.
- Only change wig style, pose, outfit, and background.
- Do not generate a different person.

Negative prompt add-on (recommended):
"different person, identity drift, face mismatch, duplicate people, extra faces, deformed face, blurry face, low quality, text, watermark, logo"

### 1. Hero Main - Premium Human Hair
Using the same exact woman from the reference photo ("a photo"), create a high-end ecommerce hero banner for a Ghana-based wig brand. She is wearing a long body-wave human hair lace front wig, natural edges, flawless makeup, and elegant black-and-gold outfit styling. Scene should feel premium and modern, with warm golden-hour light, soft shadows, subtle luxury studio background, and clean negative space on the left side for headline and CTA text overlay. Ultra-realistic commercial fashion photography, sharp focus on hair texture and shine, 16:9 composition, 4K quality. No text, no watermark, no logo.

### 2. Hero Secondary - Versatile Styles
Using the same exact woman from the reference photo ("a photo"), create a premium homepage hero image showing her in a sleek straight lace front wig with healthy shine and natural hairline. Emphasize texture detail and hair movement, with elegant editorial styling in neutral beige and deep charcoal tones plus soft gold accents in the environment. Keep center-right visual focus and leave clean negative space on the left for marketing copy. Ultra-realistic beauty campaign look, 16:9, high detail, studio-lit, 4K. No text, no watermark, no logo.

### 3. Hero Promo - Confidence and Everyday Beauty
Using the same exact woman from the reference photo ("a photo"), create a lifestyle hero banner for a premium wig and extensions store in Ghana. Show her walking through an upscale Accra-inspired urban setting, hair flowing naturally (shoulder-length lace front bob), confident expression, modern fashion, warm cinematic sunlight. Composition should feel aspirational yet accessible, with clear open space on top-left for promotional text. Ultra-realistic commercial photography, rich skin tones, realistic fabric and hair detail, 16:9, 4K. No text, no watermark, no logo.

## Category Image Prompts

### Human Hair Wigs
Create a premium category image for "Human Hair Wigs" showing a Ghanaian model with soft, natural-looking virgin human hair (body wave), close-up emphasis on healthy shine, realistic density, and natural movement. Clean studio background in warm neutral tones, luxury ecommerce style, 1:1 crop, ultra-realistic, no text.

### Lace Front Wigs
Create a category image for "Lace Front Wigs" focused on a model with a flawless HD lace frontal install and natural hairline. Show fine baby hairs and scalp melt effect in soft diffused light, premium beauty editorial style, clean uncluttered background, 1:1 crop, ultra-realistic, no text.

### Synthetic Wigs
Create a category image for "Synthetic Wigs" featuring a trendy, affordable fashion look with a Ghanaian model wearing a modern synthetic wig (defined curls or sleek ombre bob). Bright, youthful styling, clean background, vibrant but classy color grading, ecommerce-friendly composition, 1:1, ultra-realistic, no text.

### Hair Extensions
Create a category image for "Hair Extensions" showing neatly arranged premium hair bundles beside a model with long blended extensions. Highlight texture consistency, fullness, and length. Use a clean tabletop + portrait hybrid composition, warm luxury lighting, 1:1 crop, ultra-realistic, no text.

### Hair Toppers
Create a category image for "Hair Toppers" focused on practical volume and crown coverage. Show a model from slightly elevated angle with natural blending at the crown, subtle before/after storytelling feel without split-screen text. Soft natural light, minimal background, premium ecommerce style, 1:1, ultra-realistic, no text.

### Hair Accessories
Create a category image for "Hair Accessories" showing curated wig-care essentials (wig stand, edge brush, silk bonnet, adhesive, combs) arranged neatly on a premium neutral surface with soft gold accents. Clean product-focused composition, modern ecommerce styling, 1:1 crop, ultra-realistic, no text.
